### **The Stonereach Highlands (NW of Verdant Veil, W of Eryndor)**

### **Geography and Tone**

The Stonereach Highlands form a vast, windswept plateau northwest of the Verdant Veil and west of Eryndor. Jagged ridges loom over valleys carved by ancient glaciers, and deep cavern systems worm through the roots of the mountains. The weather is harsh—biting winds, sudden storms, and long winters. It is a land of endurance and grit, where the people are as unyielding as the stone they dwell upon.

Tone: **Hard, martial, resilient.** The Highlands are a borderland between civilization and wilderness, where giants still roam, wyverns circle the skies, and men and dwarves cling to their halls with stubborn pride.

---

### **Major Sites and Landmarks**

**Stonefast Hold**

* *Population*: \~2,500 (mostly dwarves and humans)

* *Description*: The largest stronghold in the region, half-buried into the cliffside. Its rune-warded gates have never fallen to giant siege. Known for its forges, mercenary contracts, and stoic rulers.

* *Notable Feature*: The *Hall of Oaths*, where mercenaries swear contracts before the altar of **Durn, the Flameforged**.

**The Goliath Steppes**

* *Description*: A windswept plateau dotted with stone monoliths and tribal cairns. Home to goliath clans that feud, raid, and occasionally ally with human highlanders.

* *Point of Interest*: The *Trial Stones*, a sacred ring of boulders where disputes are settled through contests of strength.

**Grimbarrow Peaks**

* *Description*: A ridge of serrated mountains, notorious for stone giant citadels and wyvern nests.

* *Adventure Hook*: Rumors speak of an ancient wyrm slumbering beneath the shattered peak of *Stormfang Spire*.

**The Deep Warrens**

* *Description*: A sprawling duergar enclave hidden below the mountains. Trade flows in rare gemstones and shadow-touched relics, though their allegiance is ever-shifting.

* *Hook*: Some whisper the duergar consort with agents of **Siorath, Keeper of False Truths**, spreading lies and shadow through the underworld.

**Ironmarch Keep**

* *Population*: \~800 (mostly humans and mixed mercenary bands)

* *Description*: A frontier fortress of Eryndor that anchors its western border. Known for hiring Highlanders and dwarves alike as auxiliary troops.

* *Notable Feature*: The *Bannerwall*, draped with tattered standards of giants defeated in battle.

**The Wyrmscar Valley**

* *Description*: A blasted canyon said to have been carved by the thrashings of a dying dragon centuries ago. Rich in gemstones and iron veins, but cursed with unstable caverns.

**Shrine of the Roaring Storm**

* *Description*: A mountaintop shrine to **Karveth, the Roaring Storm**. Highlanders climb the peak in storm season to offer tokens, believing Karveth grants freedom and fury in battle.

  ---

  ### **Exports and Trade**

* **Iron and Steel**: Highland forges supply Eryndor’s armies.

* **Gemstones**: Mined from dangerous caverns and disputed ridges.

* **Mercenaries**: Hardy fighters from the Highlands are in demand across the Twilight Veil, known for loyalty if well-paid.

  ---

  ### **Conflict and Adventure Potential**

* **Giant Raids**: Ongoing feuds with stone giants of the Grimbarrow Peaks.

* **Shadowed Warrens**: Duergar dealing in forbidden shadow-craft.

* **Border Wars**: Tension between Highland clans and Eryndor’s garrisons at Ironmarch.

* **Wyrm Threats**: Wyverns harassing trade caravans and whispers of an elder wyrm awakening.

* **Divine Shrines**: Sacred sites to **Durn** and **Karveth** may serve as quest locations for PCs seeking divine blessings or relics.
