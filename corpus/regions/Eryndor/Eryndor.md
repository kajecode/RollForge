# Eryndor

# Overview

**Symbol**: A golden oak tree against a silver crescent moon, symbolizing strength rooted in the land and vigilance under shadow.  
**Motto**: “Roots deep as stone, branches high as stars.”  
**Ruler**: King Altherion IV, known as the **Ever-Vigilant King**.  
**Capital**: **Eryndel**, a shining city built along a wide river, where art, culture, and strategy thrive under the shadow of a grand citadel.  
**Primary Threats**: The growing shadowy incursions from the Wyrdwood, tensions in the merchant council of Southwatch, and whispers of Flemeth’s agents stirring unrest.

# Regions

#### **The Shimmering Expanse**

* **Description**: A vast plain with rolling hills, fertile farmland, and small woodlands.  
* **Notable Settlements**:  
  * **Brindlehaven**: A quaint market village, known for its Harvest Festival and proximity to the shadowed Wyrdwood.  
  * **Alderwood**: A logging town, rugged and superstitious, sitting at the Wyrdwood's southern edge.  
  * **Riversmeet**: A bustling riverport town, vital for trade and known for its well-guarded ferries.  
  * **Emberhollow**: A mining town at the foot of the Grimfang Mountains, famed for its blacksmiths and mineral wealth.  
  * **Southwatch**: The region’s largest city, governed by a council of nobles and merchants. It’s a trade hub but rife with political intrigue.

# Key Sites

## Eryndel

**Population**: \~25,000  
**Description**: The political and cultural heart of Eryndor, situated along the Lyssara River.  
**Known For**: Governance, trade, and the Everflame Spire.  
**Details**: A bustling city with grand architecture, including the royal citadel and ancient halls of learning.  
**Notable Citizens**:

* **King Altherion IV**: The Ever-Vigilant King of Eryndor.  
* **Queen Veyra Altherion**: Diplomatic and strategic, a strong supporter of the crown.

## Goldport

**Population**: \~12,000  
**Description**: A maritime city on a large island at the Lyssara River’s mouth.  
**Known For**: Shipping, the Stormspire Lighthouse, and its vibrant marketplaces.  
**Details**: The gateway to trade across the Dawnreach Sea, bustling with activity.  
**Notable Citizens**:

* **Harbormaster Gorran Tideswell**: Oversees the city’s shipping and naval defenses.

## Ironwatch Keep

**Description**:  
Ironwatch Keep sits atop a rocky bluff between the Blackroot Fen and the Grimfang Mountains. Its iron-capped towers and sturdy battlements give it a commanding view of the surrounding plains and forest. Unlike Duskhaven, Ironwatch remains an active outpost, garrisoned by soldiers tasked with protecting Eryndor’s western border.  
**Purpose and Significance**:

* Acts as a **defensive stronghold**, ensuring safe passage between the Grimfang Mountains and the southern regions.  
* Also serves as a **beacon of hope**, symbolizing Eryndor’s resilience against shadow threats.

**Notable Features**:

* **The Iron Beacon**: A massive brazier atop the keep, used to signal distress to nearby settlements.  
* **The Hall of Vigilance**: A war room where generals once planned campaigns against shadow incursions.

## Frostmoor

**Population**: \~1,000  
**Description**:  
Nestled near the foothills of the Grimfang Mountains, Frostmoor is a rugged settlement known for its icy climate and hardy residents. Its proximity to **Frostmere Lake** makes it a hub for travelers and traders seeking to access the upper reaches of the Lyssara River.  
**Known For**:

* Fishing and ice harvesting from Frostmere Lake.  
* Serving as a waypoint for expeditions into the Grimfang Mountains.

**Details**:

* Frostmoor thrives on a mix of fishing, mining, and trade. The town square features a large, heated stone hearth called the **Gathering Flame**, where travelers warm themselves and exchange stories.

**Notable Citizens**:

* **Elder Wynn Frostbough**: A wise but reclusive leader who has deep knowledge of Grimfang’s legends and dangers.  
* **Orla Deepcurrent**: A fisher known for her tales of strange sightings in Frostmere’s depths.

## Ravenspire

**Population**: \~2,500  
**Description**:  
A fortified riverside town perched atop a steep bluff overlooking the Lyssara River. Ravenspire is a vibrant hub for trade and culture, its spire-like structures giving it an imposing yet elegant appearance.  
**Known For**:

* Its grand market, known as the **Raven’s Roost**, where traders from across Eryndor gather to exchange goods.  
* Skilled artisans, particularly jewelers and carvers, who create intricate works inspired by the surrounding wilderness.

**Details**:

* The town is heavily fortified, a remnant of its role during early shadow incursions. Its **Watchtower of Night** still stands as a reminder of its vigilance.

**Notable Citizens**:

* **Commander Erynn Haleblade**: The leader of the town guard, renowned for her loyalty to the crown.  
* **Master Artisan Doran Thistlenote**: A jeweler whose works are sought after throughout Eryndor.

## Duskhaven Citadel

Duskhaven Citadel looms above the mist-shrouded waters of the Blackroot Fen, its dark stone towers barely visible through the swamp's perpetual haze. Overgrown with creeping vines and moss, the citadel bears the scars of battles long forgotten. Once a mighty bastion of the King’s Rangers, it fell to shadow creatures during the first rise of the Shadow Sovereign.  
**Purpose and Significance**:

* Initially constructed as a **strategic outpost**, guarding Eryndor’s southern borders and controlling access through the fen.  
* Now a **haunted ruin**, its shadowed halls hold remnants of its defenders and whispers of dark magic.

**Notable Features**:

* **The Echoing Halls**: Empty corridors filled with the faint sounds of battle cries and ghostly whispers.  
* **The Blackspire Tower**: A central spire of blackened stone, believed to hold a hidden vault of Ranger relics.

## Stormwatch Keep

**Description**:  
Perched on a windswept cliff at the northeastern edge of Eryndor, **Stormwatch Keep** stands as a sentinel against external threats. Its imposing walls and storm-weathered towers are visible from miles away, symbolizing the kingdom’s vigilance.  
**Known For**:

* Defending Eryndor’s northeastern border from raiders and shadow incursions.  
* Housing the **Tempest Wardens**, an elite force trained to withstand harsh weather and fierce combat.

**Details**:

* The **Thunder Hall** within the keep echoes with the sound of wind and waves, amplifying its defenders’ battle cries.  
* The **Stormspire Tower** has magical devices to track and repel incoming threats.

**Notable Features**:

* **The Tempest Forge**: A specialized forge within the keep that produces weapons resistant to corrosion and magical storms.  
* **Signal Crystals**: Installed along the tower, they can summon aid from nearby garrisons or the Sea Sentinels of Seabreak Harbor.

## Echo Hold

**Description**:  
A vast and eerie fortress abandoned long ago, **Echo Hold** sits high in the Grimfang Mountains. Its halls are said to amplify sound, carrying whispers and footfalls into haunting echoes that make it feel alive. Once a critical military outpost for Eryndor’s early campaigns, it now lies in ruins, shrouded in legends of betrayal and shadowy incursions.  
**Known For**:

* Its **Echoing Chambers**, which unnerved even the boldest soldiers with their haunting acoustics.  
* A hidden armory rumored to hold relics of the King’s Rangers.

**Details**:

* The entrance is marked by **The Gate of Shadows**, an imposing archway carved with faded runes warning trespassers to turn back.  
* The **Chamber of Whispers** lies deep within the hold and is said to house the remains of an artifact tied to the Shadow Sovereign.

## The Dwarven Enclaves

#### **Stonehearth Enclave**

* **Population**: \~1,200 (Dwarves, with a few gnomes and halflings)  
* **Description**:  
  A thriving settlement carved into the heart of the Grimfang Mountains, **Stonehearth** is known for its masterful stonework and rich history. The enclave is a bustling center of dwarven culture, with expansive halls illuminated by glowing gemstones embedded in the walls.  
* **Known For**:  
  * Producing high-quality stonework and masonry, sought after across Eryndor.  
  * Ancestral forges that craft finely tuned mechanisms and enchanted tools.  
* **Details**:  
  * The central hall, **Hearthlight Hall**, is a grand meeting place where the council of elders governs the enclave.  
  * The **Luminary Forge**, located in the deepest cavern, is said to hold flames ignited by the ancestors themselves.  
* **Notable Citizens**:  
  * **Elder Forgekeeper Krennar Ironfist**: Overseer of the Luminary Forge and a voice of reason on the council.  
  * **Gemma Glitterspire**: A jeweler famed for her intricate gemstone carvings, often imbued with subtle magical properties.

---

#### **Irondeep Enclave**

* **Population**: \~800 (Mostly Dwarves, with a few mountain elves and humans)  
* **Description**:  
  Built deep within the mountain, **Irondeep** is renowned for its ore extraction and metallurgy. The settlement has a stark, utilitarian feel, with narrow corridors and grand, reinforced vaults.  
* **Known For**:  
  * Extracting rare ores, such as mithral and adamantine.  
  * Training some of the finest blacksmiths in Eryndor, specializing in weapons and armor.  
* **Details**:  
  * The **Hall of Anvils** is the enclave’s heart, where blacksmiths and miners work tirelessly to produce exceptional goods.  
  * Irondeep is heavily fortified, with massive iron gates and hidden defenses to guard against potential invaders.  
* **Notable Citizens**:  
  * **Master Smith Thalgar Orethane**: Known for forging blades imbued with runes of protection and sharpness.  
  * **Warden Drala Frostforge**: Head of security, a seasoned warrior who leads patrols to protect mining expeditions.

### **Settlements Along the Gold Coast**

#### **Seabreak Harbor**

* **Population**: \~5,000  
* **Description**:  
  Nestled along the craggy coastline, Seabreak Harbor is a fortified port town built on a rocky outcrop that juts into the Dawnreach Sea. The harbor is sheltered by natural cliffs, making it a safe haven for ships during storms.  
* **Known For**:  
  * A bustling fish market and its skilled shipwrights.  
  * The **Sea Sentinels**, a renowned fleet of coastal defenders.  
* **Details**:  
  * The **Waveshield Lighthouse**, standing at the harbor’s edge, is a towering beacon with magical wards to repel storms.  
  * The town square, **Breakwater Plaza**, is always alive with merchants and sailors exchanging goods and tales.  
* **Notable Citizens**:  
  * **Harbormaster Saria Tidewake**: An efficient and commanding leader of Seabreak’s port operations.  
  * **Captain Loric Saltmane**: Commander of the Sea Sentinels, known for his daring defense against pirates.

---

#### **Silverstrand**

* **Population**: \~3,500  
* **Description**:  
  A picturesque coastal town with white-sand beaches and silvery waters that shimmer under the sun. Silverstrand thrives on trade and leisure, attracting merchants and travelers seeking respite from their journeys.  
* **Known For**:  
  * Exporting pearls, salt, and fine seafood.  
  * Hosting the annual **Festival of Tides**, celebrating the ebb and flow of prosperity.  
* **Details**:  
  * The **Azure Pier** stretches far into the Dawnreach Sea, allowing large ships to dock with ease.  
  * The **Tidal Veil Tavern**, a popular stop for adventurers, is known for its ocean-themed ales.  
* **Notable Citizens**:  
  * **Mayor Ellora Seabright**: A charismatic leader who ensures the town’s prosperity through diplomacy and trade.  
  * **Merrin Brightshell**: A pearl diver with tales of strange creatures beneath the waves.

#### **Harborfall**

* **Population**: \~2,200  
* **Description**:  
  Harborfall is a thriving coastal town southeast of **Stormwatch Keep. It is** situated on a natural harbor protected by towering cliffs. Harborfall serves as a vital logistical hub, receiving goods from **Goldport** and **Seabreak Harbor** and distributing them to Stormwatch and nearby settlements. A beacon of activity, Harborfall combines efficient operations with a touch of scenic charm, making it an important fixture on the Gold Coast.  
* **Known For**:  
  * Its majestic lighthouse, **Tidefall Lighthouse**, guides ships and signals danger.  
  * A bustling port with vibrant markets and a growing reputation for shipbuilding.  
  * Strong connections to Eryndor’s northeastern defenses, ensuring steady supplies for the kingdom’s border outposts.

* **Captain Rylan Tidebreaker**: A charismatic ship captain known for daring rescues and fierce loyalty to the crown.  
* **Elder Marna Oakenwave**: A retired sailor who runs a shrine to Selûne and provides blessings to those journeying the seas.

# Government

## King Altherion IV

**Character Profile**:

* A ruler of unyielding resolve but haunted by guilt over the loss of the King's Rangers—a near-mythical order that disappeared under his reign.  
* Known for his tactical mind, King Altherion is a veteran of campaigns against orc raiders and shadowy invaders.  
* Wears the **Crown of Vigilance**, a family relic said to offer foresight in times of peril.

**Personality**:

* **Noble, but burdened**: Seeks redemption for perceived failures.  
* **Visionary**: Aims to restore the Rangers’ legacy while grappling with whispers of shadow magic corrupting the kingdom.  
* **Quietly Paranoid**: Fears Flemeth’s influence and the specter of the Shadow Sovereign.

## King’s Rangers

### **Origins**:

* Formed during the First Age of Shadows to protect the kingdom’s borders and uncover dark threats within its forests, mountains, and rivers.  
* Sworn to the motto: **"Against Shadow, With Steel, We Guard the Realm."**

### **Fate of the Rangers**:

* Approximately 25 years ago, the King’s Rangers were sent into the Wyrdwood and the Grimfang Mountains to investigate shadowy incursions.  
* The King's Rangers, stationed in **Dawnwatch Tower**, were elite protectors of the kingdom, known for their vigilance and bravery. The tower's name, inspired by their role as the kingdom’s first line of defense, symbolized hope and resilience. Their fall marked a devastating blow to the kingdom.  
* Their stronghold, **The Dawnwatch Tower**, became silent after a fateful last stand. Their disappearance allowed Vargos to seize the tower, twisting it to his purposes​The Shimmering Expanse …​Shadows over Brindlehav….

### **Legacy**:

* Songs and tales speak of their bravery, inspiring common folk. Some believe remnants of the Rangers may survive, hidden in exile or bound in service to Flemeth.

### **Hierarchy of the Rangers at Dawnwatch Tower**

#### **Leadership**

1. **Captain Ardyn Varoth**:  
   * **Role**: Master tactician and inspirational leader of the Rangers.  
   * **Fate**: Taken by Flemeth during the final battle. She twisted him into her **Shadow Hound**, a relentless hunter enslaved to her will.  
2. **Lieutenants**:  
   * **Lieutenant Caelan Halewind**:  
     * A sharpshooter with sharp wit, known for his unerring aim.  
     * **Fate**: Died protecting the northern flank of the tower during Flemeth's assault. His ghost is rumored to haunt the Wyrdwood, guarding lost Ranger graves.  
   * **Lieutenant Eryss Thornveil**:  
     * A druidic scout with deep ties to the natural world.  
     * **Fate**: Survived and now leads a band of rebels hiding in the Wyrdwood, seeking to undermine Flemeth's influence.  
   * **Lieutenant Jorath Blackstone**:  
     * A stoic warrior who led the Rangers' vanguard.  
     * **Fate**: Captured and taken to Flemeth's Grove, where his current fate remains a mystery.

#### **Support Staff**

1. **Quartermaster Myra Grendil**:  
   * **Role**: Managed supplies and provisions for the Rangers.  
   * **Fate**: Presumed dead after the fall, though rumors suggest she escaped with a vital cache of artifacts from the tower.  
2. **Cook Roderic "Red" Fellwhistle**:  
   * **Role**: A halfling who kept morale high through his cooking and stories.

   * **Role**: Crafted the enchanted blades and armor that became the Rangers’ hallmark.

### **The Fall of Dawnwatch Tower**

* **Flemeth’s Assault**:  
  * Flemeth, wielding shadow magic and commanding corrupted beasts, orchestrated the Rangers’ downfall. Her forces overwhelmed their defenses, exploiting their reliance on traditional strategies​Flemeth, Witch of the W…​The Coven of Shadow.  
  * Captain Varoth’s bravery in leading the final stand earned Flemeth’s twisted admiration. She enslaved him to her will as her Shadow Hound.  
  * The Rangers were decimated, their sacrifices delaying Flemeth long enough to spare nearby Brindlehaven from immediate destruction​Shadows over Brindlehav….  
* **Legacy**:  
  * Dawnwatch Tower now stands as a haunted ruin, corrupted by shadows and illusions. Its once-proud motto, **“Against Shadow, With Steel, We Guard the Realm,”** remains barely legible, scarred by claw marks and scorch stains.

### **Artifacts of the King’s Rangers**

#### **1\. Compass of Vigilance**

* **Description**:  
  * A small brass compass with intricate etchings of oak leaves and a crown, representing the Rangers’ motto: **“Against Shadow, With Steel, We Guard the Realm.”**  
  * The needle always points north, even in magical darkness or areas where natural navigation is impossible.  
* **Properties**:  
  * **Passive Effects**:  
    * Always points true north.  
    * Emits a faint glow in the presence of illusion or shadow magic within 30 feet.  
  * **Active Ability**:  
    * **Shadowpierce Pulse**: As an action, the wielder can expend the compass's stored energy to emit a burst of light, dispelling magical darkness and illusions in a 30-foot radius. Recharges at dawn.  
  * **Legend Lore Insight**:  
    * When used in conjunction with other Ranger artifacts, the compass reveals cryptic messages or faint glimpses of the past, potentially aiding the party.  
* **Current Location**:  
  * Recently recovered by the party in the ruins of Dawnwatch Tower. It was found in a hidden alcove beneath the captain's desk.

---

#### **2\. Steelmantle’s Blades**

* **Description**:  
  * Masterfully crafted weapons forged by Garrick Steelmantle. Each blade is engraved with runes of protection and an oak tree insignia wrapped in shadowy vines.  
  * The pommel contains a faintly glowing gem, said to resonate with the wielder's determination.  
* **Properties**:  
  * **Enchantment**: \+1 to attack and damage rolls.  
  * **Glowing Edge**: The blade emits a faint silvery light in the presence of shadow creatures or illusion magic within 20 feet.  
  * **Defensive Stance**:  
    * When the wielder takes the Dodge action, the blade grants temporary hit points equal to 1d4 \+ their proficiency bonus.  
  * **Legend Lore Insight**:  
    * The blade vibrates faintly when near a hidden Ranger artifact or Flemeth's shadow-infused creations.  
* **Current Location**:  
  * One of these blades has been found by the party in the Spire’s ruins, still lodged in the body of a shadow beast near the forge Garrick defended in his final moments.  
  * Others may remain scattered, either in Ranger graves or hidden caches.

---

#### **3\. Shadowbane Bow**

* **Description**:  
  * A sleek longbow crafted from enchanted ashwood, reinforced with steel accents. The bowstring appears to shimmer faintly as if woven from moonlight.  
  * Its name, "Shadowbane," is etched in Elvish along the upper limb of the bow.  
* **Properties**:  
  * **Enchantment**: \+1 to attack and damage rolls.  
  * **Illusion Breaker**:  
    * Arrows fired from this bow deal an additional 1d6 radiant damage to shadow creatures or targets benefiting from illusions.  
  * **Revealing Shot**:  
    * Once per short rest, an arrow fired from Shadowbane can dispel illusions or magical darkness within 10 feet of its target on impact.  
  * **Legend Lore Insight**:  
    * The bow whispers faint phrases in Elvish when held, often cryptic warnings or guidance.  
* **Current Location**:  
  * Held by **Lieutenant Eryss Thornveil**, one of the few surviving Rangers. She has been hiding in the Wyrdwood, leading a small band of rebels against Flemeth’s agents.  
  * **Eryss's Intent**:  
    * Eryss believes Shadowbane is meant to find its way into the hands of a worthy champion—someone capable of standing against Flemeth or the rising threat of Siorath. She is waiting for such an individual to prove themselves in combat or through their dedication to the Rangers' ideals.  
  * **Encounter Idea**:  
    * The party may stumble upon Eryss while exploring the Wyrdwood. She might test their worth by assigning them a dangerous task, such as cleansing a corrupted shrine or defeating one of Flemeth's shadow-twisted creatures.
