# Southwatch — City of Masks and Rivers

### Overview
Southwatch is the greatest city of the southern riverlands, standing proudly where the **Shimmerflow** and **Moonshadow Rivers** merge into the mighty **Lyssara**. Built on stone terraces above the waterline, it thrives on commerce, river trade, and its famed traditions of masked festivals.

By day, Southwatch is a place of bustling markets and guild halls; by night, lanterns shimmer over canals and plazas, and masks turn the city into a stage of revelry and mystery.

---

## Districts of Southwatch

- **Moonshadow District**  
  The old heart of the city, a maze of alleys, shrines, and teahouses.  
  - **Moonshadow Walk:** Famous for its mask-makers, courtesans, and entertainers.  
  - **Lanternmarket:** A night market filled with glowing paper lanterns, active year-round but at its peak during festivals.  

- **Lyssara Docks**  
  A cacophony of ferries, barges, and merchant ships from all over Eryndor.  
  - **Rivermouth Docks:** The busiest wharves, home to shipwrights and warehouses.  
  - **Fisher’s Quay:** Where local fishermen unload their catches at dawn.  

- **Lawkeeper’s Row**  
  Center of law, order, and contracts.  
  - **Bondsmen’s Hall:** Headquarters of the Guild of Bonds and Bounties.  
  - **Justice Plaza:** The site of public hearings and proclamations.  

- **Highmarket District**  
  The city’s grand market quarter, alive with color and trade.  
  - **Silkweaver’s Row:** An arcade where Anari silk and exotic fabrics are sold.  
  - **Council Square:** A plaza where criers, petitioners, and merchants mingle under the shadow of the Council Quarter.  

- **Council Quarter**  
  The political heart of Southwatch.  
  - **Council Keep:** A fortress-like hall where the city’s council meets.  
  - **Golden Promenade:** A boulevard of noble estates and embassies.  

- **Sanctuary District**  
  Spiritual center of the city.  
  - **Sanctuary of the Everbloom:** The great temple of Thalyssia, the Verdant Mother.  
  - **Verdant Plaza:** A green market square known for apothecaries, herbalists, and gardeners.

---

## Life in the City
- **Masks and Festivals:** Southwatch is famed for its **Masquerade Festival**, but masks are common all year — in markets, courts, and taverns.  
- **Markets and Trade:** Exotic goods arrive daily — silk, dwarven steel, enchanted trinkets. Bargaining is sharp, and masks make every deal a gamble.  
- **Rivers and Docks:** The rivers define the city. Bargemen, fishers, and ferrymen are as much a part of Southwatch’s rhythm as merchants and nobles.

---

## Points of Interest
- **Council Keep** — Seat of government.  
- **Sanctuary of the Everbloom** — Largest temple of the south.  
- **Masks of the Market** — A plaza of artisans who sell shifting enchanted masks.  
- **Aqueduct Arches** — Ancient riverworks admired as feats of old engineering.  

---

## Governance
The **Council of Southwatch** rules the city, made of merchants, nobles, and advisors. High Priestess Isolde Merrowyn, though not a councilor, wields influence equal to a seat.

---

### The Spirit of Southwatch
Southwatch is a city of dual faces: **trade and opportunity by day, masks and intrigue by night.**
