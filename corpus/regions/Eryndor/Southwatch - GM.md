# Southwatch — GM Reference

### Strategic Importance
Southwatch commands the **triple confluence** of the Shimmerflow, Moonshadow, and Lyssara Rivers, controlling inland trade from Eryndel to Goldport. Whoever shapes Southwatch shapes the riverlands.

---

## Districts & Hidden Hooks

- **Moonshadow District**  
  A warren of alleys and shrines.  
  - **Moonshadow Walk:** Veiled Hand safehouses are hidden among mask-makers and courtesans.  
  - **Lanternmarket:** Popular night market; Syndicate spies often trade coded messages here.  
  *Hook:* PCs may intercept shadow cult whispers disguised as festival songs.

- **Lyssara Docks**  
  The city’s arteries of trade — and crime.  
  - **Rivermouth Docks:** Controlled by the **Blackthorn Syndicate**, a hub for smuggling contraband and cursed relics.  
  - **Fisher’s Quay:** Disappearances here are tied to river cults of Siorath.  
  *Hook:* The Nightbloom Market emerges monthly under the aqueduct — a perfect place for shady deals.

- **Lawkeeper’s Row**  
  Home to the **Guild of Bonds and Bounties**, keepers of writs and bounty contracts.  
  - **Bondsmen’s Hall:** PCs may seek (or be targeted by) official bounties.  
  - **Justice Plaza:** Ideal for intrigue or public challenges.  
  *Hook:* Rival guilds manipulate trials to discredit enemies.

- **Highmarket District**  
  The crown of Southwatch’s commerce.  
  - **Silkweaver’s Row:** Anari silk merchants often hide smuggled poisons or shadow reagents.  
  - **Council Square:** Orators and criers mask political manipulation with performance.  
  *Hook:* Council agents and Syndicate enforcers both use the plaza as neutral ground.

- **Council Quarter**  
  Political heart of the city.  
  - **Council Keep:** Currently destabilized by Margrave Hadrin Kestell’s disappearance.  
  - **Golden Promenade:** Nobles whisper alliances; Syndicate couriers move in masked processions.  
  *Hook:* Perfect for assassination attempts or stolen-vote intrigue.

- **Sanctuary District**  
  Spiritual core, but not free of corruption.  
  - **Sanctuary of the Everbloom:** Priests notice leyline corruption surfacing in rituals.  
  - **Verdant Plaza:** Apothecaries sometimes peddle reagents tainted by shadow influence.  
  *Hook:* PCs may uncover Siorath’s cult blending with herbalists and alchemists.

---

## Factions at Play
- **Veiled Hand (Selanwë):** Spy network manipulating truths; operates from the Crescent House in Moonshadow Walk.  
- **Blackthorn Syndicate:** Controls smuggling; their Nightbloom Market under Rivermouth Docks is infamous.  
- **Guild of Bonds and Bounties:** Legal but political; often weaponized by councilors.  
- **Arcantheum Envoy (Aetheris):** Watches for magical contraband and poaches talent.  

---

## Cult of Siorath
- Hidden shrines beneath the Aqueduct Arches and even within false façades of other temples.  
- Masks split between male/female faces mark their rituals.  
- Goal: undermine trust in both gods and council, corrupting leyline nodes beneath the delta.

---

## Adventure Hooks
1. **The Vanishing Quay:** Fisherfolk vanish — cultists use river caverns for rites.  
2. **Market of Masks:** A cursed mask circulates during the Lanternmarket festival.  
3. **Council Intrigue:** Gerald Thornewell bribes Bondsmen to silence rivals; PCs must expose the fraud.  
4. **Nightbloom Market:** PCs must infiltrate or bargain with Syndicate smugglers beneath Rivermouth Docks.  
5. **Shadowed Leyline:** Corruption seeps into the rivers; the Sanctuary calls for a ritual to cleanse it.

---

### Tone
Southwatch is a **city of masks and whispers**: every district has a face for the public, and another hidden beneath.
