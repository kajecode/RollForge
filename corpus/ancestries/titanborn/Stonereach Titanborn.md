# **Stonereach Titanborn**

*Children of stone and storm, Titanborn are towering warriors who dwell among the peaks and valleys of the Stonereach Highlands. Their lineage is traced back to ancient unions of mortal clans and stone giants, tempered by centuries of survival in a realm of wyverns, giants, and endless storms. They embody endurance, tradition, and a martial pride that marks them as living bastions of the Highlands.*

---

### **Titanborn Traits (5e)**

Your Titanborn character has the following traits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 1\.

**Age.** Titanborn mature slightly faster than humans, reaching adulthood at 15\. They live up to 120 years.

**Alignment.** Titanborn tend toward lawful or neutral alignments, guided by clan traditions and oaths.

**Size.** Titanborn stand between 7 and 8 feet tall and weigh 280 to 350 pounds. Your size is Medium.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

**Mountain’s Endurance.** You have resistance to cold damage.

**Stone’s Grip.** You have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks made to climb or to resist being shoved or grappled.

**Highland Toughness.** Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 again whenever you gain a level.

**Storm’s Challenge.** Once per long rest, when you hit a creature with a melee attack, you may issue a thunderous challenge. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 \+ your proficiency bonus \+ your Strength modifier) or be frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Giant.

---

### **Subraces**

Titanborn clans vary in their heritage and traditions. Choose one of the following subraces.

---

#### **Stonebound**

Titanborn closest to the mountain roots are massive and stoic, considered the “old blood.”

**Stone’s Resilience.** Once per long rest, when you take bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage, you can reduce the damage by 1d12 \+ your Constitution modifier.

---

#### **Stormborn**

These Titanborn dwell on wind-scoured ridges and are said to be favored by Karveth, the Roaring Storm.

**Stormcaller.** You know the *gust* cantrip.  
 At 3rd level, you can cast *thunderwave* once per long rest. Constitution is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

---

#### **Wyrmscarred**

Descended from clans marked by ancient battles with wyverns and dragons, their skin often bears ridged, scale-like patterns.

**Draconic Resistance.** Choose one damage type: acid, fire, lightning, or poison. You have resistance to that damage type.

---

### **Titanborn Culture**

* **Clans & Oaths.** Titanborn clans keep carved Oath-stones in their halls; breaking a sworn oath is said to curse one’s bloodline.

* **Mercenary Tradition.** Many Titanborn hire out as warriors across the Twilight Veil. To them, service in battle is a test of worth, not servitude.

* **Faith.** Most Titanborn honor Durn, the Flameforged, and Karveth, the Roaring Storm, though wyrmscarred clans sometimes whisper prayers to Nytheris.

* **Rites of Passage.** Youth prove their strength by climbing the Trial Stones on the Goliath Steppes during a storm, bare-handed and unaided.
