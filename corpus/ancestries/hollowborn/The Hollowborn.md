# The Hollowborn

## A Reimagined Warforged Race for the Twilight Veil

---

#### **Origins of the Hollowborn**

The **Hollowborn** are ancient constructs, blending magic and engineering, created by a forgotten civilization desperate to cheat death. These beings were crafted as vessels to house the **lifesparks** of their creators—soul essences meant to escape the ravages of mortality. However, their genesis is a tale of ambition turned tragic:

1. **The Lifespark Project:**  
   * Crafted from enchanted metals and runed wood, the husks were designed to house the essence of a soul, granting immortality. Citizens were chosen by lottery to undergo the process, though wealthier individuals often bribed their way in.  
   * The transfer process drew on arcane energy sources unknown to the modern world. These intricate rituals tethered the soul to the husk, theoretically granting eternal life.  
2. **The Cataclysm:**  
   * During the height of the project, a catastrophic failure in the arcane network created a rippling disaster. The event shattered the memories of the stored lifesparks, reducing them to blank slates. The facility was encased in ice as the surrounding region fell into ruin.  
   * The Hollowborn were left dormant in their husks, locked in suspended animation for centuries.  
3. **Rediscovery:**  
   * Centuries later, explorers unearthed the frozen facility. Curiosity and accidental triggers caused the ice to fracture, releasing the Hollowborn from their stasis.  
   * Awakening with no memory of their past lives, they emerged as individuals with a second chance at life, bearing no connection to the civilization that created them.

---

#### **Physical Traits of the Hollowborn**

The Hollowborn are living constructs, appearing as a blend of ancient artistry and arcane technology:

* **Bodies:** Intricate frames of enchanted metals and polished, rune-inscribed wood. Each Hollowborn's body is unique, reflecting the craftsman who created it.  
* **Cores:** A visible glowing core in their chest, known as the **lifespark**, pulses faintly with energy. This core sustains their vitality and is the key to their existence.  
* **Eyes:** Hollowborn eyes glow softly, often matching the color of their lifespark (hues of blue, green, amber, or violet).  
* **Aging:** They do not age or decay like organic beings, but prolonged inactivity or damage requires magical repair.

---

#### **Cultural Traits of the Hollowborn**

1. **Blank Slates:**  
   * The Hollowborn have no memory of their prior lives or the civilization that created them. They are driven to define themselves in the new world, often struggling with existential questions about their purpose and identity.  
2. **Reaction from Society:**  
   * While many view the Hollowborn as marvels of lost technology, some fear them as remnants of a dangerous age. Superstitions often cast them as harbingers of the same hubris that caused the Cataclysm.  
   * Their rarity and mysterious nature make them objects of curiosity in academic circles and targets for exploitation by shadowy factions.  
3. **A Deep Instinct for Exploration:**  
   * Hollowborn are inherently drawn to ruins, ancient artifacts, and arcane phenomena, as though remnants of their creators' ambitions still guide their actions.

---

#### **Racial Traits for Gameplay**

* **Type:** Construct (Living Construct subtype).  
* **Lifespan:** Functionally immortal, though stasis or inactivity can occur.  
* **Size:** Medium.  
* **Speed:** 30 feet.  
* **Lifespark Resilience:** Immune to disease and resistant to poison damage. Cannot be magically aged.  
* **Adaptive Physique:** Rest for 4 hours in a meditative state instead of sleeping for 8 hours. This state repairs minor damage and allows reflection, akin to dreaming.  
* **Memory Echo:** Gain proficiency in one skill of your choice (a faint trace of a forgotten past life).  
* **Soulbound Core:** The Hollowborn's lifespark sustains their existence. When reduced to 0 hit points, they must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or their lifespark dims, rendering them incapacitated until repaired.  
* **Elemental Affinity:** Choose one damage resistance at character creation: cold, fire, or lightning. This reflects remnants of the facility’s arcane energies.

---

#### **Role in the Twilight Veil**

The Hollowborn embody the themes of the **Twilight Veil**: mystery, rebirth, and the consequences of hubris. Their presence provides rich opportunities for storytelling:

* **Discovery of the Facility:** The remnants of the frozen facility may hold clues to their origins and the lost civilization.  
* **Existential Struggles:** Hollowborn characters grapple with their identity, finding purpose in a world that neither created nor understands them.  
* **Shadowy Interest:** Factions tied to shadow or arcane research may seek to exploit Hollowborn as relics of a powerful past.
