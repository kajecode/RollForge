# **Grung — The Venom-Blooded**

### **Origins & Homeland**

The **Verdant Veil swamps**, the **marshes of Moonfang Cove**, and the **fetid lagoons of the Jade Canopy** are home to the Grung — small, brightly colored amphibians with alien minds and alien laws. Their society is bound by an unyielding **caste system**, where a Grung’s skin color determines its role, rights, and destiny. To outsiders, they are strange, insular, and dangerous — equal parts elusive hunter, venomous priest, and communal dreamer.

---

### **Culture & Society**

* **Rigid Castes.** A Grung’s place is determined at birth by color:

  * **Green:** Warriors, hunters, and laborers.

  * **Blue:** Artisans, domestic workers, and childcare. Their crafts are traded in frontier markets.

  * **Purple:** Overseers of the green and blue. They enforce harmony and discipline.

  * **Red:** Scholars, shamans, and priests. Masters of magic and respected even by higher castes.

  * **Orange:** Elite warriors, protectors of sacred places, and commanders of lesser grungs.

  * **Gold:** Chieftains and war-leaders. All tribes are ruled by gold grung.

* **Communal Rituals.** Grung sing in circles beneath the moon, working themselves into trances that reaffirm caste bonds.

* **Venom-Craft.** Their alchemical poisons are feared across the Twilight Veil. Mishandled, they can kill as easily as they can empower.

* **Alien Worldview.** Outsiders often find Grung utterly strange: what is instinct to them — caste, venom, ritual — feels oppressive and incomprehensible to others.

---

### **Religion & Belief**

* **Animism.** Grung appease swamp-spirits of water, venom, and the moon.

* **Totemic Worship.** Sacred idols daubed in venom and swamp mud embody these spirits.

* **Moonlight Ties.** Some believe Selanwë’s silver gaze guides their wanderlust and breeding cycles.

---

### **Relations with Others**

* **Verdant Veil Druids.** Cautious respect, though druids condemn Grung reliance on venom.

* **Lizardfolk & Orcs.** Trade and rivalry both flourish, often over territory and tribute.

* **Anari & Tabaxi.** Rarely allied, sometimes trading, but suspicion is mutual.

* **Humans & Others.** Most see Grung as dangerous swamp-dwellers; few trust them.

---

## **Grung Traits (5e)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score by \+2 and another by \+1, or three different scores by \+1 each.  
 **Age.** Grung mature within a year of hatching and live up to 50 years.  
 **Alignment.** Their caste-bound societies tend toward lawful alignments, though exiles and wanderers often rebel against that order.  
 **Size.** Grung stand 2½ to 3 feet tall and weigh 25–30 pounds. Your size is Small.  
 **Speed.** Your base walking speed is 25 feet. You also have a climbing speed of 25 feet.

---

### **Racial Traits**

**Arboreal Alertness.** You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

**Amphibious.** You can breathe air and water.

**Standing Leap.** Your long jump is up to 25 feet and your high jump is up to 15 feet, with or without a running start.

**Water Dependency.** If you fail to immerse yourself in water for at least 1 hour during a day, your skin dries and you suffer the poisoned condition at the end of that day. This condition lasts until you immerse yourself in water for 1 hour or receive magical healing.

**Poison Resilience.** You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance to poison damage.

**Grung Poison.** You can exude venom from your skin and apply it to a melee weapon or natural attack. On a hit, the target takes 2d4 poison damage and must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC \= 8 \+ your proficiency bonus \+ your Constitution modifier) or suffer an additional effect based on your caste color.

* You can use this feature **once per long rest at 1st level, twice per long rest at 5th level, and three times per long rest at 11th level, four times per long rest at 16th level and five times at 20th.**

**Caste Effects:**

* **Green.** The target’s speed becomes 0 until the end of its next turn.

* **Blue.** The target is distracted; it has disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

* **Purple.** The target is disoriented; until the end of its next turn, it must use all its movement to move at least 5 feet if possible.

* **Red.** The target is compelled to waste its action; until the end of its next turn, it must use its action to interact with an object (such as drawing a weapon or picking up debris).

* **Orange.** The target becomes frightened of one ally of your choice until the end of its next turn.

* **Gold.** The target is charmed by you until the end of its next turn. While charmed, it can understand and speak Grung.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Grung.

---

### **Roleplay Notes**

* **Color is Destiny.** Your skin marks your caste. Will you embrace your role, or defy it?

* **Alien Mindset.** Outsiders rarely understand you — do you bridge the gap or remain inscrutable?

* **Venom is Sacred.** Do you treat your poison as a tool, a blessing, or a curse?

* **Clan or Exile.** Most Grung adventurers are exiles, defectors, or chosen emissaries.
