# **Tabaxi — Moonlit Wanderers**

### **Origins & Homeland**

Tabaxi are nomadic clans of feline folk who roam the **Jade Canopy**, especially along the borders of **Moonfang Cove** and the volcanic slopes of **Emberwake**. They travel in **canoe-caravans** woven of reeds and vines, climbing the canopy with ladders and rope-bridges, rarely settling in one place for long. Their lives are steeped in oral tradition, carrying the memory of the **fallen empire buried beneath the jungle**.

---

### **Culture & Society**

* **Nomadic Caravans.** Tabaxi travel in extended family bands, moving along rivers, coastlines, and canopy routes. Their villages are temporary — woven into trees or lashed to canoes.

* **Storykeepers.** They preserve history through song, tale, and mask — storytellers are revered as much as hunters.

* **Trade & Curiosity.** Known as scouts, guides, and traders, tabaxi deal in lore as much as goods. They are welcome in markets, but regarded with suspicion for their sudden arrivals and departures.

* **Mystique.** Outsiders see them as mysterious wanderers, often misjudged as fickle or aloof when their curiosity drives them.

---

### **Religion & Belief**

* **Moonlit Path.** Tabaxi revere **Selanwë, the Moon-Maiden**, whose silver light they believe guides wanderers and storytellers.

* **Dream-Hunters.** They interpret omens in moonlight reflected on water, and many carry moon-charms as protective talismans.

* **Lore of the Empire.** Their stories tell of the fallen jungle empire — some tabaxi dedicate their lives to unearthing its secrets.

---

### **Relations with Others**

* **Anari.** Cautious respect; the spiderfolk guard their secrets, but trade silk for lore.

* **Grung.** Distrustful — the grung caste rigidity clashes with tabaxi freedom.

* **Humans.** Tabaxi are often exoticized in cities like Southwatch and Eryndel, where they are hired as scouts or guides.

* **Orcs & Lizardfolk.** Relations shift between rivalry and alliance depending on territory.

---

## **Tabaxi Traits (5e)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score by \+2 and another by \+1, or three by \+1 each.

**Age.** Tabaxi mature quickly, reaching adulthood by 12, and live up to 80 years.

**Alignment.** Tabaxi lean toward chaotic alignments, guided by curiosity and freedom.

**Size.** Medium (5½–6½ ft., 100–160 lbs.).

**Speed.** 30 ft.

**Darkvision.** 60 ft.

**Feline Agility.** When you move on your turn, you can double your speed until the end of that turn. Once you use this trait, you can’t use it again until you move 0 feet on one of your turns.

**Claws.** You have retractable claws that you can use as natural weapons. They deal 1d4 slashing damage.

**Cat’s Talent.** You have proficiency in the Stealth and Perception skills.

**Languages.** Common and Tabaxi.

---

### **Roleplay Notes**

* **Wanderlust.** Your clan may be on pilgrimage, in exile, or seeking old truths — what drives your travels?

* **Moonlight Faith.** Do you honor Selanwë openly, or keep your devotion quiet?

* **Storykeeper.** Every tabaxi carries stories; some are gifts, others are burdens. Which tales shape you?

* **Mistrusted Wanderer.** In cities, you are both admired and distrusted. Do you lean into the mystique, or work to prove yourself?
