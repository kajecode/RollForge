# **Kobolds — Children of the First Brood**

### **Origins & Homeland**

Kobolds are small, industrious reptilian folk who thrive in the **caves of the Stonereach Highlands** and the **volcanic tunnels of Mount Xalthek** in the Jade Canopy. Their warrens are labyrinths of twisting tunnels, choke-points, and traps, designed as much for survival as defense. Though often dismissed as pests, kobolds see themselves as sacred — chosen **keepers of dragonkind’s bloodlines**, bound to the **First Brood of Primal Wyrms**.

---

### **Culture & Society**

* **Warrens.** Life centers on sprawling tunnel-complexes carved into stone and ash. Each warren is a fortress, mine, and shrine in one.

* **Clan & Status.** Leadership is based not on wealth or might, but on signs of draconic favor — unusual scales, visions, or prophetic omens.

* **Invention & Traps.** Ingenious, if dangerous, kobold traps protect their homes and serve as both art and weapon.

* **Draconic Obsession.** Every kobold sees themselves as a fragment of dragonkind’s destiny. To serve a dragon, or emulate one, is the greatest honor.

  ---

  ### **Religion & Belief**

* **The Primal Wyrms.** Kobolds venerate six ancestral wyrms who hatched from the World-Egg before the Sundering:

  * **Solkara, Flame of the Sun** (Fire, Renewal).

  * **Lunareth, Pale Serpent of the Moon** (Cycles, Shadow).

  * **Stellara, Star-Eyed Dragon** (Magic, Fate).

  * **Thamros, Earthfather** (Stone, Guardianship).

  * **Aelion, Sky-Wind** (Freedom, Storms).

  * **Ozythra, Sea-Mother** (Depth, Eternity).

* **Shrines.** Each warren tends six effigies, offering coal, moonwater, gems, stone dust, incense, and saltwater to bless newborn eggs.

* **Radical Sects.** A minority follow **Tiamat** or other patrons (Baphomet, Ravok), but most kobolds scorn them as betrayers of the Brood.

  ---

  ### **Relations with Others**

* **Dragons & Dragonborn.** Revered as kin and masters.

* **Goblinoids.** Practical trade partners, exchanging ore and traps for steel or mercenaries.

* **Dwarves & Humans.** Mistrusted and hunted, but grudgingly respected for craft and survival.

* **Anari & Tabaxi.** Uneasy coexistence at jungle borders; kobold warrens sometimes overlap with Anari ruins or Tabaxi caravan routes.

  ---

  ## **Kobold Traits (5e, Twilight Veil Variant)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score by \+2 and another by \+1, or three by \+1 each.

**Age.** Mature by 6, live up to 120 years.

**Alignment.** Kobolds lean lawful, guided by clan traditions and draconic devotion.

**Size.** Small (2½–3½ ft., 25–35 lbs.).

**Speed.** 30 ft.

**Darkvision.** 60 ft.

**Draconic Legacy.** Choose one:

* **Craft.** Gain proficiency with one artisan’s tool.

* **Traps.** Gain proficiency with Sleight of Hand or Investigation.

* **Magic.** Learn one Sorcerer cantrip (Charisma \= spellcasting ability).

**Draconic Roar.** As a bonus action, you let out a draconic cry. Until the start of your next turn, you and allies within 10 feet gain advantage on attack rolls against enemies you can see. Uses \= proficiency bonus per long rest.

**Sunlight Sensitivity (Optional).** Some kobold clans suffer disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks relying on sight in sunlight.

**Languages.** Common, Draconic.

---

## **Kobold Sub-Lineages — Scions of the First Brood**

When a kobold comes of age, their scales or dreams may reveal which Primal Wyrm’s blood sings in them. These lineages shape their powers, omens, and destinies.

### **🔥 Solkara-Blooded (Children of the Sun-Flame)**

* **Fire Affinity.** You know the *produce flame* cantrip.

* **Blazing Courage.** Once per long rest, when you deal fire damage, add your proficiency bonus to the damage roll.

* **Temperament.** Passionate, proud, quick to burn hot.

  ---

  ### **🌙 Lunareth-Blooded (Children of the Pale Serpent)**

* **Shadow Veil.** You know the *minor illusion* cantrip.

* **Cycle’s Trick.** When making a Stealth check in dim light or darkness, add \+1d4.

* **Temperament.** Prophets, spies, and shadow-binders.

  ---

  ### **✨ Stellara-Blooded (Children of the Star-Eyed)**

* **Spark of Magic.** Learn one Sorcerer cantrip (your choice). Charisma \= spellcasting ability.

* **Starlit Insight.** Once per long rest, reroll one failed Arcana or Insight check.

* **Temperament.** Curious and fate-marked, sometimes eccentric.

  ---

  ### **🪨 Thamros-Blooded (Children of the Earthfather)**

* **Stone’s Fortitude.** Once per long rest, when you take damage, reduce it by 1d8 \+ Con modifier.

* **Burrow Instinct.** Proficiency in Athletics or Mason’s Tools.

* **Temperament.** Loyal guardians and secret-keepers.

  ---

  ### **🌪 Aelion-Blooded (Children of the Sky-Wind)**

* **Storm Affinity.** You know the *gust* cantrip.

* **Rebel’s Surge.** Once per long rest, when you use Draconic Roar, push one creature of your choice within 10 feet back 10 feet.

* **Temperament.** Free-spirited, rebellious, and adventurous.

  ---

  ### **🌊 Ozythra-Blooded (Children of the Sea-Mother)**

* **Ocean’s Gift.** You know the *shape water* cantrip.

* **Hunger of the Depths.** Once per long rest, when you hit a creature, force it to make a Strength save (DC \= 8 \+ PB \+ Con mod) or be pulled 10 feet toward you.

* **Temperament.** Both nurturing and terrifying, feared and revered.

  ---

  ### **Roleplay Notes**

* **Clan or Brood?** Do you serve your warren, or the greater legacy of dragons?

* **Marked by Wyrm.** Does your lineage bring pride, duty, or danger?

* **Survivalist Mind.** Small but cunning, kobolds win through wit, traps, and sheer numbers.

* **Faith in the First Brood.** To you, dragons are more than beasts — they are the living echoes of creation.
